Notes from Katie

KATIE: Research Questions

  1. To what extent do SGMY report bystander intentions in situations of TDV and RAU?

  2. What are the associations between TDV bystander intentions relate to RAU bystander intentions?

  3. To what extent do TDV and RAU bystander intentions relate to:

    1. demographic/social positioning variables (i.e., age, gender, sex assigned at birth, sexual orientation, and race/ethnicity),

    2. TDV victimization and perpetration, alcohol use, perceptions of social norms specific to alcohol use and TDV, depression, emotional dysregulation, and population-specific factors (e.g., internalized homo/bi/trans phobia, outness, and LGBTQ+ sense of community). 

IVs: BYpru & BYreu & LTI

Interested Variables:

  1. age

  2. gender

  3. sex-at-birth

  4. SexOri

  5. race

  6. TDV variable

  7. ACOMu - Alcohol consequences

  8. perceptions of social norms specific to alcohol use and tdv

  9. depression

  10. emotinoal dysregulation

  11. internalized negativity/phobia

  12. outness

  13. LGBTQ+ sense of community

Analyses

Research Question 1:

  1. To what extent do SGMY report bystander intentions in situations of TDV and RAU?

We were confused as to exactly what Katie was looking for here. RAU stands for Risky Alcohol Use we interpreted this as ACOMuR (Alcohol Consequence). Here’s how we interpreted the question:

(1) Those who report any TDV, what is their level of Bystander intervention (BYpru/BYreu)?

(2) Those who report any ACOMuR, what is their level of Bystander Intervention (LTI)?

(3) Is there a significant relationship between TDV and BYreu/BYpru?

(4) Is there a significant relationship between ACOMuR and LTI?

As such proposed analyses are:

Linear Regression

  • Di-chotomized TDV & BYreu

  • Di-chotomized TDV & BYpru

  • Di-Chotomized ACOMur & LTI

Cross-table

  • Di-BYreu & Di-TDV

  • DI-BYpru & Di-TDV

  • Di-LTI & ACOMuR (Alcohol Consequences in Past 3 months)

Descriptives

  • Report level of BYpru for those who have TDV

  • Report level of BYreu for those who have TDV

  • Report level of LTI for those who have ACOMuR
